Awab Holdings – Elevator Pitch

We are a Saudi Arabian, family-owned company with a global reach; our network and business interests extend throughout the Middle East, Europe and North America.

We are uniquely positioned to pursue investment opportunities around the world, however, our capacity to look outwards in no means disconnects us from our roots.

Awab Holdings is a next-generation investment vehicle for the Alotaibi family to meaningfully realise their ambitions in venture capitalism, innovation, and philanthropy.

Awab Holdings has a wide range of expertise operating several subsidiaries, including Cepco Medical Services, Jubail Motors and Codeit, as well as Awab Real Estate and Trading, while concurrently managing a diversified investment portfolio.

Our capacity to extend our reach as both passive and active investors has never been greater. Reinvigorating conventional business models with an eye to the future is the ongoing pursuit of Awab Holdings. We're open to new and innovative value propositions — Awab Holdings wants to be part of the next big idea.
